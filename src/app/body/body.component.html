Same great taste, same great food, same great service, and same great family atmosphere for the past 40 years!

The previous owner stayed on board to teach him everything about the business, including food, ingredients, and preparation techniques for all the dishes on the menu. All the sauces, dough, lasagna, ziti, etc are homemade the same way they have been for the past 40 years.
